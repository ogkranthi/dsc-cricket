## The Numbers

DSC's 2025 campaign across four tournaments tells the story of a team that showed up consistently:

| Tournament | Played | Won | Lost | Position |
|-----------|--------|-----|------|----------|
| MCL T20 | 12 | 8 | 4 | 3rd |
| DBL T20 | 11 | 8 | 3 | 2nd |
| DBL T10 | 11 | 5 | 6 | 7th |
| MCL Fall T16 | 6 | 5 | 2 | 2nd |
| **Total** | **40** | **26** | **15** | **65% Win Rate** |

Four tournaments, four semifinal appearances. That's consistency.

## Season Highlights

Two moments stood out above the rest, both voted equally by the squad:

**Somu's 5 sixes in 5 balls** — Somasekhar Mamidipaka's extraordinary assault that left the opposition shell-shocked. Pure carnage.

**Low scoring thriller vs Nashua Warriors** — A match that proved cricket isn't just about big scores. Nerves, skill, and grit decided this one.

## Team Performance

The squad rated the overall team performance at **7.9 out of 10**, with captaincy and leadership earning an impressive **8.1/10**. 13 out of 16 core members felt satisfied with fair playing chances — a strong indicator of the inclusive culture Kranthi and the leadership group have built.

## Key Positives

- **Semifinal consistency** — reaching the knockout stage in all four tournaments
- **Team camaraderie** — the spirit in the dressing room was the season's biggest win
- **T20 dominance** — the MCL T20 and DBL T20 campaigns (combined 16-7) showed DSC belong at the top
- **Defending low scores** — several memorable wins protecting modest totals
- **Exciting run chases** — the team showed composure under pressure repeatedly

## Looking Ahead

The squad identified clear areas for growth heading into 2026:

**Batting consistency** — establishing fixed batting lineups with clear role definitions rather than rotating positions each game. More emphasis on ground shots and building innings.

**Practice structure** — fixed weekly schedules with targeted drills. The team wants vision-based feedback technology for technique analysis.

**Squad depth** — addressing availability issues and building a larger reliable core to reduce last-minute scrambles.

The team overwhelmingly approved an internal DSC tournament for the offseason, along with bonding activities and a potential YouTube channel for match highlights.

With four semifinal campaigns behind them and a 65% win rate, DSC's 2025 was a season to be proud of. The foundations are laid — 2026 is about converting semifinals into finals.
