The 2026 season is upon us, and DSC is gearing up for what promises to be their most exciting campaign yet.

## Squad Strength

The team returns with a strong core — Ravi Kumar's explosive batting at the top, Vikram Singh's all-round brilliance, and Amit Sharma's fiery pace make DSC a formidable outfit. The addition of new players to the squad adds depth and competition for places.

## Key Fixtures

The season opener against Central Tigers on March 15 will set the tone. A home fixture at Central Park gives DSC the advantage, and the team will look to start on a winning note.

## Season Goals

Captain Kranthi has set clear targets: "We want to win the league this year. We have the squad, the belief, and the hunger. Every match matters."

## What to Watch

- **Ravi Kumar's form** — Can he maintain last season's incredible strike rate?
- **New ball partnership** — Amit Sharma and the new seamers need to make early inroads
- **Spin twins** — Sunil Patel and Vikram Singh in the middle overs could be match-winners
